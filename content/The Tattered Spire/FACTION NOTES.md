So, there are a handful of factions that have started being put on the players' radar. Writing down my thoughts on what all these sponsors/factions want so that I can point to clear goals

### Palestone
Alliance between Palestone, Bismuth, Gravesbones, Ikot

Wants:
- Stay in power
- Return to 'ultimate' power as sole leadership of their city
### Bismuth
Alliance between Palestone, Bismuth, Gravesbones, Ikot

Wants:
- Oust Duergar in any form.
	- Sending them back underground would be mostly sufficient in their minds
- Stay in power
	- Keep Divineu leadership mostly dwarven
	- Stay a 'Clan' or move to 'Bloodline'
### Duergar
Allied with most of the Crestative families.

Wants:
- Palestone lineage killed
- Bismuth lineage killed
- Other races in leadership
	- Removal of Crested and Crestative distinctions
	- Removal of Bloodline, Clan, Family distinctions
	- Unified voting body based on fair representationship of the city
### Ikot
Allied directly to Palestone, indirectly to Bismuth, and Gravesbones

Wants:
- More power over the Courts
- Their family members in higher standing
### Torken

Wants:
- To become the ruler of Diveneu
	- Doesn't care for a Council
	- Fine with being the shadow pulling strings
		- MUST not have pushback on decisions aside from anything more than 'milord, that's not advisable'
- His legacy to live on in his family
### Opti (by extension, Shadowbind)

Wants:
- Wealth beyond comprehension
- Azure to take notice of him
- Leadership of the financial side of Diveneu
	- Start some sort of merchants guild thats required of all those doing trade in Diveneu
	- Enforce his empire via the Thieves' guild he has started
