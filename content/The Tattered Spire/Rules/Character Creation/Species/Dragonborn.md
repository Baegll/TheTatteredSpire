Dragonborn in The Tattered Spire, are an intentional creation by [[Furzhold M Groust]] they are created as humanoid versions of the dragon species. First created as soldiers for [[23 The Scale War (SAS)|The Scale War]] they are few in number now, especially due to their outlawed nature in most cities and towns. Dragonborn share the same naming title as the color of dragon they hail from, however lowercase to denote their humanoid status.

**Creature Type:** Humanoid 
**Size:** Medium (about 5–7 feet tall) 
**Speed:** 30 feet

As a Dragonborn, you have these special traits.

**Draconic Ancestry.** Your lineage stems from a dragon progenitor. Choose the kind of dragon from the [[#Table Draconic Ancestors|Draconic Ancestors table]]. Your choice affects your [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] and Damage [[Damage and Healing#Resistance and Vulnerability|Resistance]] traits as well as your appearance.

**Breath Weapon.** When you take the [[Actions#Table Actions|Attack action]] on your turn, you can replace one of your attacks with an exhalation of magical energy in either a 15-foot Cone or a 30-foot Line that is 5 feet wide (choose the shape each time). Each creature in that area must make a [[The Six Abilities|Dexterity]] saving throw (DC 8 plus your [[The Six Abilities|Constitution]] modifier and [[Proficiency]] Bonus). On a failed save, a creature takes 1d10 damage of the type determined by your Draconic Ancestry trait. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage. This damage increases by 1d10 when you reach character levels 5 (2d10), 11 (3d10), and 17 (4d10).

You can use this [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] a number of times equal to your [[Proficiency]] Bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a [[Long Rest]].

**Damage Resistance.** You have [[Damage and Healing#Resistance and Vulnerability|Resistance]] to the damage type determined by your Draconic Ancestry trait.

**Darkvision.** You have [[Darkvision]] with a range of 60 feet.

**Draconic Flight.** When you reach character level 5, you can channel draconic magic to give yourself temporary flight. As a [[Actions#Bonus Actions|Bonus Action]], you sprout spectral wings on your back that last for 10 minutes or until you retract the wings (no action required) or have the [[Incapacitated]] condition. During that time, you have a [[Fly Speed]] equal to your [[Speed]]. Your wings appear to be made of the same energy as your [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]]. Once you use this trait, you can't use it again until you finish a [[Long Rest]].
