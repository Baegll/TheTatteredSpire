_2nd level Enchantment (Cleric, Paladin, Warlock, Wizard)_

**Casting Time**: 1 Action
**Range**: 60 feet
**Components**: V, S, M (a testament of holy virtues)
**Duration**: Instantaneous

You utter a word of condemnation against a creature within range. The target must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or take 4d6 radiant damage and become restrained by golden chains until the end of its next turn. While restrained in this manner, the target is affected by the following Conditions: [[Blinded]], [[Deafened]], [[Incapacitated]], [[Restrained]], [[Revealed]] and [[Stunned]]. In addition, the target is unable to teleport or shift planes of existence.

**At Higher Levels**: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can affect one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd. The creatures must be within 30 feet of each other when you target them.
