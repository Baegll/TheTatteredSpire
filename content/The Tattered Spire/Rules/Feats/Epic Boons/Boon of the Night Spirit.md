*Prerequisite: Level 19+*

**Ability Score Increase**. Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.
**Merge with Shadows**. While within [[Exploration#Light|Dim Light]] or [[Exploration#Light|Darkness]], you can give yourself the Invisible condition as a [[Actions#Bonus Actions|Bonus Action]]. The condition ends on you immediately after you take an action, a [[Actions#Bonus Actions|Bonus Action]], or a [[Actions#Reactions|Reaction]].  
**Shadowy Form**. While within [[Exploration#Light|Dim Light]] or [[Exploration#Light|Darkness]], you have Resistance to all damage except [[Psychic]] and [[Radiant]].
