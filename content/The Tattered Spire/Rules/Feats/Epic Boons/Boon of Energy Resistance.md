*Prerequisite: Level 19+*

**Ability Score Increase**. Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.
**Energy Resistances**. You gain [[Damage and Healing#Resistance and Vulnerability|Resistance]] to two of the following damage types of your choice: [[Acid]], [[Cold]], [[Fire]], [[Lightning]], [[Necrotic]], [[Poison]], [[Psychic]], [[Radiant]], or [[Thunder]]. Whenever you finish a [[Long Rest]], you can change your choices.
