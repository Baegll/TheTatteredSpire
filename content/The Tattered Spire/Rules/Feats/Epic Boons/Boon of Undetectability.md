*Prerequisite: Level 19+*

**Ability Score Increase**. Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.
**Hidden Visage**. You gain a +10 bonus to [[The Six Abilities|Dexterity]] ([[Proficiency#Skill Proficiencies|Stealth]]) checks, and you can't be detected or targeted by divination magic, including scrying sensors.
