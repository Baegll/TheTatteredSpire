*Prerequisite: Level 19+*

**Ability Score Increase**. Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.  
**Blink Steps**. Immediately after you take the [[Actions#Table Actions|Attack action]] or the [[Actions#Table Actions|Magic action]], you can teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see.
