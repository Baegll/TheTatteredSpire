**Initiative Proficiency**. When you roll [[Combat#Initiative|Initiative]], you can add your [[Proficiency]] Bonus to the roll.  **Initiative Swap**. Immediately after you roll [[Combat#Initiative|Initiative]], you can swap your [[Combat#Initiative|Initiative]] with the [[Combat#Initiative|Initiative]] of one willing ally in the same combat. You can't make this swap if you or the ally has the [[Incapacitated]] condition.
