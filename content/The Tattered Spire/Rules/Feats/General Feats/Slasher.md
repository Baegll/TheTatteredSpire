**Ability Score Increase**. Increase your [[The Six Abilities|Strength]] or [[The Six Abilities|Dexterity]] score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
**Hamstring**. Once per turn when you hit a creature with an attack that deals [[Slashing]] damage, you can reduce the [[Speed]] of that creature by 10 feet until the start of your next turn.  
**Enhanced Critical**. When you score a [[Damage and Healing#Critical Hits|Critical Hit]] that deals [[Slashing]] damage to a creature, it has [[D20 Tests#Advantage/Disadvantage|Disadvantage]] on attack rolls until the start of your next turn.
