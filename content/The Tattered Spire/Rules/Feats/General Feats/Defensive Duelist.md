**Ability Score Increase**. Increase your [[The Six Abilities|Dexterity]] score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
**Parry**. If you're holding a [[Finesse]] weapon and another creature hits you with a melee attack, you can take a [[Actions#Reactions|Reaction]] to add your [[Proficiency#Table Proficiency Bonus|Proficiency Bonus]] to your Armor Class, potentially causing the attack to miss you. You gain this bonus to your AC against melee attacks until the start of your next turn.
