*Prerequisite: Level 4+, Dexterity 13+*

**Ability Score Increase**. Increase your [[The Six Abilities|Dexterity]] score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
**Bypass Cover**. Your ranged attacks with weapons ignore [[Combat#Cover|Half Cover]] and [[Combat#Cover|Three-Quarters Cover]].  
**Firing in Melee**. Being within 5 feet of an enemy doesn't impose [[D20 Tests#Advantage/Disadvantage|Disadvantage]] on your attack rolls with [[Ranged weapon|Ranged weapons]].  
**Long Shots**. Attacking at long range doesn't impose [[D20 Tests#Advantage/Disadvantage|Disadvantage]] on your attack rolls with [[Ranged weapon|Ranged weapons]].
