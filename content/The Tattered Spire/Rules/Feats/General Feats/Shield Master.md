*Prerequisite: Level 4+, Shield Training*

**Ability Score Increase**. Increase your [[The Six Abilities|Strength]] score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
**Shield Bash**. If you attack a creature within 5 feet of you as part of the [[Actions#Table Actions|Attack action]] and hit with a [[Melee weapon]], you can immediately bash the target with your [[Shield]] if it's equipped, forcing the target to make a [[The Six Abilities|Strength]] saving throw (DC 8 plus your [[The Six Abilities|Strength]] modifier and [[Proficiency]] Bonus). On a failed save, you either push the target 5 feet from you or cause it to have the [[Prone]] condition (your choice). You can use this benefit only once on each of your turns.  
**Interpose Shield**. If you're subjected to an effect that allows you to make a [[The Six Abilities|Dexterity]] saving throw to take only half damage, you can take a [[Actions#Reactions|Reaction]] to take no damage if you succeed on the saving throw and are wielding a [[Shield]].
