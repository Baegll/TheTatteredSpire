*Prerequisite: Level 4+, Medium Armor Training*

**Ability Score Increase**. Increase your [[The Six Abilities|Strength]] or [[The Six Abilities|Dexterity]] score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
**Dexterous Wearer**. While you're wearing [[Medium Armor]], you can add 3, rather than 2, to your AC if you have a [[The Six Abilities|Dexterity]] score of 16 or higher.
