*Prerequisite: Level 4+, Charisma 13+*

**Ability Score Increase**. Increase your [[The Six Abilities|Charisma]] score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
**Impersonation**. While you're disguised as a real or fictional person, you have [[D20 Tests#Advantage/Disadvantage|Advantage]] on [[The Six Abilities|Charisma]] ([[Proficiency#Skill Proficiencies|Deception]] or [[Proficiency#Skill Proficiencies|Performance]]) checks to convince others that you are that person.  
**Mimicry**. You can mimic the sounds of other creatures, including speech. A creature that hears the mimicry must succeed on a [[The Six Abilities|Wisdom]] ([[Proficiency#Skill Proficiencies|Insight]]) check to determine the effect is faked (DC 8 plus your [[The Six Abilities|Charisma]] modifier and [[Proficiency]] Bonus).
