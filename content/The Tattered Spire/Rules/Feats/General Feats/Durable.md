*Prerequisite: Level 4+*

**Ability Score Increase**. Increase your [[The Six Abilities|Constitution]] score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
**Defy Death**. You have [[D20 Tests#Advantage/Disadvantage|Advantage]] on Death Saving Throws.  
**Speedy Recovery**. As a [[Actions#Bonus Actions|Bonus Action]], you can expend one of your Hit Point Dice, roll the die, and regain a number of Hit Points equal to the roll.
