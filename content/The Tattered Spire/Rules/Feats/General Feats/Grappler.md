*Prerequisite: Level 4+, Strength or Dexterity 13+*

**bility Score Increase**. Increase your [[The Six Abilities|Strength]] or [[The Six Abilities|Dexterity]] score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
**Punch and Grab**. When you hit a creature with an [[Unarmed Strike]] as part of the [[Actions#Table Actions|Attack action]] on your turn, you can use both the Damage and the [[Grappling|Grapple]] option. You can use this benefit only once per turn.  
**Attack Advantage**. You have [[D20 Tests#Advantage/Disadvantage|Advantage]] on attack rolls against a creature [[Grappled]] by you.  
**Fast Wrestler**. You don't have to spend extra movement to move a creature [[Grappled]] by you if the creature is your size or smaller.
