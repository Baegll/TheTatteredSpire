*Prerequisite: Level 4+, Intelligence 13+*

**Ability Score Increase**. Increase your [[The Six Abilities|Intelligence]] score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
**Lore Knowledge**. Choose one of the following skills: [[Proficiency#Skill Proficiencies|Arcana]], [[Proficiency#Skill Proficiencies|History]], [[Proficiency#Skill Proficiencies|Investigation]], [[Proficiency#Skill Proficiencies|Nature]], or [[Proficiency#Skill Proficiencies|Religion]]. If you lack proficiency in the chosen skill, you gain proficiency in it, and if you already have proficiency in it, you gain [[Expertise]] in it.  
**Quick Study**. You can take the [[Actions#Table Actions|Study Action]] as a [[Actions#Bonus Actions|Bonus Action]].
