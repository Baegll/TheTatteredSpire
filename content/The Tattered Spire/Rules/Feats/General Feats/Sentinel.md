*Prerequisite: Level 4+, Strength or Dexterity 13+*

**Ability Score Increase**. Increase your [[The Six Abilities|Strength]] or [[The Six Abilities|Dexterity]] score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
**Guardian**. Immediately after a creature within 5 feet of you takes the [[Actions#Table Actions|Disengage Action]] or hits a target other than you with an attack, you can make an [[Combat#Opportunity Attacks|Opportunity Attack]] against that creature.  
**Halt**. When you hit a creature with an [[Combat#Opportunity Attacks|Opportunity Attack]], the creature's [[Speed]] becomes 0 for the rest of the current turn.
