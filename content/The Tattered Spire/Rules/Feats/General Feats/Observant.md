*Prerequisite: Level 4+, Intelligence or Wisdom 13+*

**Ability Score Increase**. Increase your [[The Six Abilities|Intelligence]] or [[The Six Abilities|Wisdom]] score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
**Keen Observer**. Choose one of the following skills: [[Proficiency#Skill Proficiencies|Insight]], [[Proficiency#Skill Proficiencies|Investigation]], or [[Proficiency#Skill Proficiencies|Perception]]. If you lack proficiency with the chosen skill, you gain proficiency in it, and if you already have proficiency in it, you gain [[Expertise]] in it.  
**Quick Search**. You can take the [[Actions#Table Actions|Search Action]] as a [[Actions#Bonus Actions|Bonus Action]].
