*Prerequisite: Level 4+, Dexterity or Constitution 13+*

**Ability Score Increase**. Increase your [[The Six Abilities|Dexterity]] or [[The Six Abilities|Constitution]] score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
**Speed Increase**. Your [[Speed]] increases by 10 feet.  
**Dash Over Difficult Terrain**. When you take the [[Actions#Table Actions|Dash action]] on your turn, Difficult Terrain doesn't cost you extra movement for the rest of that turn.  
**Agile Movement**. [[Combat#Opportunity Attacks|Opportunity Attacks]] have [[D20 Tests#Advantage/Disadvantage|Disadvantage]] against you.
