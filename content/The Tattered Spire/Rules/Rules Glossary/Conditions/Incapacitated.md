While you have the Incapacitated condition, you experience the following effects.

**_Inactive._** You can't take any [[Actions#Table Actions|action]], [[Actions#Bonus Actions|Bonus Action]], or [[Actions#Reactions|Reaction]].

**_No Concentration._** Your [[Concentration]] is broken. 

_Speechless._ You can't speak.

**_Surprised._** If you're Incapacitated when you roll [[Combat#Initiative|Initiative]], you have [[D20 Tests#Advantage/Disadvantage|Disadvantage]] on the roll.
