While you have the Restrained condition, you experience the following effects.

**_Speed 0._** Your [[Speed]] is 0 and can't increase.

**_Attacks Affected._** Attack rolls against you have [[D20 Tests#Advantage/Disadvantage|Advantage]], and your attack rolls have [[D20 Tests#Advantage/Disadvantage|Disadvantage]].

**_Saving Throws Affected._** You have [[D20 Tests#Advantage/Disadvantage|Disadvantage]] on [[The Six Abilities|Dexterity]] saving throws.
