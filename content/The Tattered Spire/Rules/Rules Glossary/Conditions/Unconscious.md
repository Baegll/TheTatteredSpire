While you have the Unconscious condition, you experience the following effects.

**_Inert._** You have the [[Incapacitated]] and [[Prone]] conditions, and you drop whatever you're holding. When this condition ends, you remain [[Prone]].

**_Speed 0._** Your [[Speed]] is 0 and can't increase.

**_Attacks Affected._** Attack rolls against you have [[D20 Tests#Advantage/Disadvantage|Advantage]].

**_Saving Throws Affected._** You automatically fail [[The Six Abilities|Strength]] and [[The Six Abilities|Dexterity]] saving throws.

**_Automatic Critical Hits._** Any attack roll that hits you is a [[Damage and Healing#Critical Hits|Critical Hit]] if the attacker is within 5 feet of you.

**_Unaware._** You're unaware of your surroundings.
