## Description
The halfling landlady who won the Brass Lantern in a card game from [[Nideno Larmanteau]]. She has a supernatural ability to appear wherever trouble is about to start, usually with a loaded crossbow she calls 'The Negotiator.' Shes also the only one who knows what's actually in [[Aldrin Larmanteau|Aldrin]]'s 'Neutral Solution' and refuses to tell anyone.
