## Description
An elderly gnome who claims to be writing the definitive history of the city "from the bottom up". He pays for drinks with small prophecies that are annoyingly accurate but uselessly specific. His numerous notebooks are written in a cipher that changes weekly, and he's paranoid someone will steal his work.
