## Description
A very obvious fence who always operates from the corner booth, always facing the door. He earned his nickname "Copper" from his habit of biting every coin to test it, including copper pieces. Despite his profession, he has a soft spot for orphans and you learn really quickly he funds a soup kitchen named "Tangles".
