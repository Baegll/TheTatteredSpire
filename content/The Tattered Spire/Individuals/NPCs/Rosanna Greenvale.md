## Description
The owner of the tavern, the bookkeeper, and unofficial historian. She's been keeping track of every tab, bet, and promise made in the Griffin for twenty years. She has a running bet board on everything from "next noble to be caught in a scandal" to "number of teeth Thessaly will knock out this month."
