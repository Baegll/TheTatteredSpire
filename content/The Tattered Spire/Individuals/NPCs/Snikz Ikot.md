## Description
A wiry, fluffy feathered blue kobold draped in too-long robes with a clumsily tailored bone clasp with several white feathers. His especially large eyes are wide, and a slightly mismatched shade of blue.
### Personality
They speak with theatrical gestures, exaggerated pauses, and an extensive vocabulary that only half makes sense. Snikz is utterly convinced he is either a god or a legendary wizard trapped in a "temporary kobold phase"
