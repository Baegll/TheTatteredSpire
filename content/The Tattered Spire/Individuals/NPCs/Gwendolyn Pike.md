## Description
A former pit fighter turned baker, she got the nickname 'Knuckles' from her habit of kneading dough with the same intensity she once brought to the ring. Her scarred hands produce the most delicate pastries which are only served at the Lantern, she delivers fresh bread to the Lantern every day, and stays for several hours enjoying the Maggie's company before the Inn gets busy.
