## Description
A [[Colossal]] [[The Black Dragon|Black Dragon]] towering above buildings at an impressive 40' height. His feathers shimmer sickly, and dark like oil on water. His horns used to be formed into the perfect crescent as typical for a black dragon, however due to his striking down in [[23 The Scale War (SAS)|The Scale War]] the right horn is cracked and bent upwards at an odd angle. 
### Personality
An extremely solitary creature, wicked, and conniving, who seeks only himself atop a throne of the world.
### History / Background
The terrible black dragon Furzhold attempted to control the world with his legions of [[Dragonborn]] in [[23 The Scale War (SAS)|The Scale War]].
