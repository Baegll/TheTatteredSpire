## Description
A very tall half-orc woman who works as a pseudo bouncer most nights. She's selectively mute, communicating through an elaborate system of hand signals that the regulars have attempted to learn how to interpret. She's also the tavern's unofficial champion at arm wrestling, having never lost a match in five years. While not officially paid, she is given free room and board by the owners of the tavern for her services.
