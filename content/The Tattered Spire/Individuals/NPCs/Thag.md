## Description

Thag is the owner of the [[HornEater Pub]]. Tall for a dwarf, and nearly as wide as he is tall. He has thick unruly black hair, only one eye, and is missing nearly every tooth on the left side of his face with a large scar on his cheek telling half the story.

His black hair is tied back behind his head in a single pony tail that it puffs out of right after the binding, mixing into the great mass of hair are a few shocks of grey. His great black beard is showing scattered shocks of grey hair as well

He is missing all but the furthest back teeth on the left side of his face, and has a very large scar running along the left side of his mouth as well. These are leftover reminders of a time when he was hit square in the face with a mace as a younger man.

Though he is a dwarf, he stands at a surprising 5'9", and is built like a brick house. Thick arms wrapped in corded muscle gently clean the surface of the bar nearly all hours of the day, and he speaks with a subtle but deep baritone.
