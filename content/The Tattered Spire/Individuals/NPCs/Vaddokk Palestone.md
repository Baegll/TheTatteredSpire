## Description
First son of [[Thraegar Palestone]] who married [[Hethos Golzak]] in a marriage of convenience in [[44 The Founding of Diveneu (SAS)]] to grant peace to [[Diveneu]].

Raised within the stark grandeur of the Palestone halls in [[Mt. Veridian]]. Tempered by the cunning and steel of fighting the [[The Golzak Tribe|Golzak]] line warriors. He carries the burdens of both history and expectation. Broad-shouldered and white haired like [[The Mines of Verdin]]. Vaddokk is known for his measured words, granite resolve, and careful planning. 

His death in [[157 The Stone War (SAS)|The Stone War]] was a primary reason [[The Serpentin Clan]] was [[The Serpentin's Folly|hunted to extinction]]. He and his second wife [[Embes Golzak|Embes]] were killed by [[Monistal Serpentin]].
