## Description
A priest of Embria who claims the tavern as his "secondary temple." His followers believe enlightenment can be found at the bottom of a cup, though Aldric seems to need many cups to find it. He performs weddings at the tavern for those who can't afford temple ceremonies and has a surprisingly beautiful singing voice when he's exactly three ales in.
