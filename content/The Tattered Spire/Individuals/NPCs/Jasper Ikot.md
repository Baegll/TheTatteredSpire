## Description
This small green feathered kobold is a bard who is terrible at music, but an amazing storyteller. He's been banned after the "lute incident" but captures the attention of everyone in the evening with his tales which may or may not be true. He claims to have dated a dragon, founded three kingdoms, and invented soup.
