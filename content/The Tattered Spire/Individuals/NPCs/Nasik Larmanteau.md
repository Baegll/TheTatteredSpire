## Description
Sharp-tongued, she manages the Larmanteau family's actual business interests. She conducts meetings at the Lantern specifically because it annoys their father. She keeps a running tally of how many marriage proposals she's rejected at the bar (currently at 47) marked on the beam beside her regular table. Despite her ruthless reputation, she secretly pays the tabs of many veteran soldiers who drink there.
