## Description
A young man, and failed alchemist who lost his sense of smell in an experiment gone wrong. He frequents the Lantern because its the only place that serves his invented cocktail: "The Neutral Solution." A gray, tasteless drink he insists has proper nutritional value and gets you the proper amount of drunk. He judges all food and drink by texture alone now, leading to bizarre recommendations.
