

## Introduction

"You'll know you're in the Steel District just as soon as your eyes start burning from the sting of smoke and coal ash.."

This section of the city is ever covered in a thick haze of smoke, coal ash, and various fumes from refining processes. Accompanying this is the ever present sound of hacking, whooping coughs from the unlucky few who come down with sickness from exposure to the vile air of the place. 

"If there is at least one thing to like, it never gets cold here, and that's a fact.."

Forge fire is seen frequently on both sides of the street. The pounding of hammers threatens to knock down your eardrums, just as the smoke assails your eyes and lungs. But what else do you expect from one of the few true industrial districts on the continent.
