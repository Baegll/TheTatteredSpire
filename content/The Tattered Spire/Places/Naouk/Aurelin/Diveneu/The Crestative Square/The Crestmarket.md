In [[The Crestative Square]], [[The Crestative Families]] display their House Banners. In that same space, the Crestmarket serves as the commercial heart for the aspiring nobility and skilled craftspeople. The market radiates outward from the central square in concentric rings, with the most prestigious stalls closest to the banner displays. Ambitious and competitive, the merchants constantly try to outdo each other with superior goods and services.
