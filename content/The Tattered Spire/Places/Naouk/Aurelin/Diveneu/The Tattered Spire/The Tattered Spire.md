A glittering silvery grey stone tower stands tall through the mountain range. Its peak supported by five long thin pillars which hold aloft a flat dias known as [[The Kreig]]. [[The Tattered Spire]] is the clear landmark of history and a beacon of power for the city of [[Diveneu]]. The tower itself, despite its grand stature, has a remarkably unkempt [[Palestone]] finish. It's said the tower itself is held together by divine threads of fate, [[Ley-Lines]] extend out from the tower, just like the many branching paths of [[The White Roads]] that facilitate trade to [[Diveneu]]. 

Here, travelers come to enter its main hall known as [[The Silver Archives]] where the libraries of [[The Crested Families]] are open to the public. No other floors of the Spire are open to the public. Though the knowledge of these floors is common. [[The Dragon's Core]], [[The Chronarium]], [[The Divine Hall]] are all considered base floors of the tower. The upper levels are known as [[The Resonance Hall]], [[The Berin Vault]], and [[The Echoes Gallery]]. The crest of the spire is split into five chambers, each with a large exposed staircase leading to [[The Kreig]]. These five chambers are known as [[The Zenith]], [[The Fate]], [[The Embrace]], [[The Conduit]], and [[The Breath]]

The only way to get to the other floors of the Spire is by ancient knowledge passed down from [[Naoplem Sterlay]], an Astral Sorcerer credited with manifesting the Spire with the help of the Ancient Silver Dragon known as [[Regannia Ag Rokreig]]. This knowledge is only passed from the head of [[The Crested Families]] families to their next of kin.
