Housing the honorable dead, there are a handful of entrances to The Catacombs in the city, often with posted paladin guards from [[The Gravesbones Family]]. The most notable entrancing being [[The Grand Netandiel Palace]] at the foot of [[Mt. Oryndral]]. The Catacombs are multi-layered and extend far down below into [[The Underdark]] and [[The Under-Diven]]
