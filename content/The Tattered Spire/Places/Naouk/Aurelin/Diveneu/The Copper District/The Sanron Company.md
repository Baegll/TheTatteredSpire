Founded and operated by the influential [[The Sanron Family]], The Company is a well-oiled mercantile and logistical engine for adventurers, nobles, and explorers alike. Located in the bustling heart of [[The Copper District]] across the street from [[The Copper Pocket]], their headquarters is a stone tomb of record-keeping, magical contract binding, and constant negotiation. In addition, a simple wooden board outside of the building is constantly being updated with new hot jobs to entice new workers to come into the building and take upon themselves.

The company is equal parts adventuring guild, insurance syndicate, and a broker of influence. Functioning as an easy go-between for upstart adventurers and the aristocratic web between commoners and the [[The Crested Families|Crested]] and [[The Crestative Families|Crestative]] families. Whether youre looking to earn glory, fulfill an obscure noble's hunt for [[Bismuth Horn Fragments]], or collect a bounty on a local criminal. The Sanron Company is your first stop.

### Core Services
- Expedition Logistics
	- Organizes adventurers, merchants, and nobles to coordinate and supply various parts of the party.
- Bounty Board Management
	- Maintains the bounties of local criminals, and assess the danger level of key criminals with the help of [[The Duergar Clan]] operating as servants to the city guard.
- Talent Acquisition
	- Finds, and maintains a retinue of adventurers for nobles to contact for key jobs.
- Sanron Scoring
	- Loosely tracks and rates adventurers to ensure as few deaths happen under their quests.
