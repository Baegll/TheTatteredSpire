Located next to [[The Sanron Company]] is the famed Beast & Bone, a sprawling and fragrant butcher-mage shop run by [[The Torken Family]]. While next door [[The Sanron Company|The Company]] sends adventurers out, Beast & Bone ensures every kill comes home with profit. Beast & Bone also operates as a general butchery for non-magical animals, often hunters in the local mountains will only trust the illustrious [[Caro Torken|Mr Torken]] to carve their kills into delicious cuts of meat.

### Core Services
- Butchery
	- General kill and cut for all sorts of beasts, magical and non-magical.
- Vivariums
	- Live-Capture holding pens for both preserving exotic pets and for training new mounts for battle.
- The Meat Shop
	- Front of house for selling common goods.
