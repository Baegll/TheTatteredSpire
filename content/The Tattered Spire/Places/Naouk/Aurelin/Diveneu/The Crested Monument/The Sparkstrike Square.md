Located at around [[The Crested Monument]], this prestigious market caters to the nobility and wealthy merchant class. This 'market' has stalls outside of shops featuring the finest goods that money can buy. Stalls are permanent structures of carved stone and brass, each bearing the merchant's crest or guild symbol. Operating on strict regulations enforced by representatives from [[The Slagsmoke Family]] guards.
