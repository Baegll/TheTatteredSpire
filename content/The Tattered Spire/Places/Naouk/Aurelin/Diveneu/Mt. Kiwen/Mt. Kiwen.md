# Introduction

Known as the "Mountain of Promises", [[Mt. Kiwen]] is revered by those who seek the blessings of [[Velthara]], [[Dealine]], and [[Silviana]].

During certain times in the [[Yehr]], those standing upon [[The Palemuth Bridge]] may gaze toward the peak of [[Mt. Kiwen]] at night and witness rare alignments of the moons: [[Alvorden]], [[Neton]], and [[Zeaf]]. Most of the time, only two moons partially overlap, creating a modest spectacle. On rare occasions, all three moons intersect in a partial alignment. Rarest of all is the moment when they fully overlap — a sight considered nothing short of miraculous.

Devotees of [[Silviana]] seek deep meaning in these lunar convergences. The specific combination of intersecting moons is only one facet among many in the intricate art of celestial divination. Every alignment is studied in context — position, duration, and the phase of each moon — forming a complex web of omens and interpretations.

Many promises are made on [[The Palemuth Bridge]]. Beyond its natural symbolism of connection, the bridge’s viewpoint with the intersecting moons makes it an auspicious place to exchange binding agreements.

# Geography
The upper half of [[Mt. Kiwen]] was once composed primarily of a soft, black stone susceptible to erosion. Over time, wind and rain has worn down this surface layer, exposing harder rock beneath. This process created natural terraces of black sand along the slopes — formations the locals call “beaches.”

# Traditions
#### [[Palestone]] Mania
In certain beaches, shimmering grey flecks of [[Palestone]] can be seen glinting against the dark sands. Though their presence doesn’t signal a true deposit, many opportunist sift through the sands in search of the precious stone. Whenever a sizable [[Palestone]] is discovered, a [[Palestone]] Mania ensues. The most famous being the [[Palestone Mania of 180]].

#### Lovers' Binding
For the most lovestruck, devotion leads them on a pilgrimage to the summit of [[Mt. Kiwen]]. There, beneath the open sky and among the black sand beaches, couples write their names into the sand and wait for the wind to carry them away. It is said that [[Dealine]] gathers these names and records them in a celestial registry, sealing the union for eternity.

The climb to [[Mt. Kiwen]] is not an easy one. While hiring escorts is strongly recommended, some lovers—eager to prove their commitment—venture up unprepared, driven more by passion than caution.
