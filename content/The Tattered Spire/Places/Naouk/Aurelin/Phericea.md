

## Introduction

Sitting at the base of the [Divine Mountain Range] upon the rocky ground is the industrial powerhouse of [Aurelin], Phericea. The [Steel District] glows day and night, fed raw materials from the ancient rediscovered [Saxur Mine]. The people of the city are a tough crowd, low on manners and high on disdain for "Those pixies up top the mountain". It can be a hard life working the forges, coal plants, or mines of the city, but ale at the [HornEater Pub] is strong enough to take down the even the burliest miner.

The surrounding forests are full of lumberjacks gathering trees to feed the ever hungry charcoal factories. There is beautiful red strata on display in most of the rocks and surrounding caves due to the high iron content of the region, and an ever present hint of smoke filing the air limiting visibility throughout the forest.

## History of Phericea

The ruins left behind by those who came before where discovered many long years ago, tattered remnants of grand stone buildings. Massive structures speaking of an age of architectural and engineering prowess. But for all the grandeur of the ruins left behind, there is precious little information about the original inhabitants of the city. 

Now in the modern day you can see the cities leading factions and houses have built their homes and guild building on the bones of the ruins left behind. An odd mix of monolithic stone blocks, and more modern, smaller stone and wood, building methods. Towering buildings and manses fill the inner square of the city.

### Discovery of the Saxur Mine

Phericea first started being re-settled roughly 200 [Yehrs] ago by hunters and trappers. Then one night a hunter was making camp in a small cave not too far from the city, when she discovered odd markings in old Dwarvish on the walls. Upon further inspection, she realized this small cave opened up to a large cavernous chamber, filled with decrepit and crumbling mining equipment. Her name was [Aelia Saxur]. Through long years of renovating the mines, and re-opening the collapsed entrance she was able grow wealthy and invest in the small settlement of Phericea, therefore forming the noble [House Saxur].

## In the modern day

The current Mayor of Phericea is [Malicus Saxur] who is also the current head of House Saxur. The city has not always kept with this tradition though. The populace is practical and hold little loyalty to leaders they see as weak or unwilling to get their hands dirty. There is one rival house in Phericea who at times is able to take leadership when a weaker Saxur is head of the house. The other house is [House Kiwen] an ore merchant house who decided to make Phericea their new home some 100 yehrs ago.
