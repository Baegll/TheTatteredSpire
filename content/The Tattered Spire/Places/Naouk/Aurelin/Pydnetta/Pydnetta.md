## Intro
The sprawling low land fields of Pydnetta is the home to many wayward vagabonds seeking to experience the Laughing Bloom. Famous for the Pydnetta flower that the city derives its namesake from, Pydnetta is less a city more than it is a ramshackle of settlements on stilts due to explosive population growth in the past 50 [[Yehr|Yehrs]].

Despite the areas fertile soil, Pydnetta has been sparsely populated for much of history due to frequent storms from the west seas and runoff from the surrounding mountains that would waterlog the plains.

However, sometime after [[66 The Obsidian Eclipse (SAS)|The Obsidian Eclipse]] many began to notice a striking violet flower that contrasted against the rest of the plains. No one is quite sure how it came to be. However, powerful [[Zedancial]] druids feel an imprint in the immaterial world so deeply scarring they are able to have a glimpse to what happened...

## Birth of the Pydnetta
During [[66 The Obsidian Eclipse (SAS)|The Obsidian Eclipse]], a clan of [[Gnome|Gnomes]] living in the plains that worshipped [[Zedancial]] felt a dreamy aspect of [[Neton]] envelope the land like a warm blanket over a toddlers eyes. To some in the clan, the endless night dulled their senses and were convinced that they were trapped in an eternal dream. To others, they felt a deeper connection to [[Zedancial]] and thought it a permanent state of euphoria. However, both sides were in agreement that a higher level of consciousness was needed to wake up or fully embrace the dream state.

Like the start of many eclectic [[Zedancial]] rituals, this would involve hallucinatory effects. To refine a hallucinogen deemed powerful enough for their purpose, the Gnomes set about enchanting a flower that would be transformed into the Pydnetta.

The Pydnetta absorbed the energy from the Gnomes over the days of the ritual. At the end, when the light of the sun broke through [[Neton]], the now bulbous Pydnetta was disturbed and burst - releasing a potent hallucinogen. Now, the Pydnetta has spread across the valley. It lies dormant, sucking up a lot of water throughout the year and blossoms after heavy rainstorms because the darkness of the rainstorm mirrors the conditions of the eclipse.
## Pydnetta (flower)
The Pydnetta is a small flower that establishes a deep root system. During the frequent heavy storms in the area, the Pydnetta will absorb large amounts of water and nutrients until it swells and forms a bulbous sack. After a short period of time, the sack will burst and release a mild hallucinogen into the air that locals now call the [[Laughing Bloom]] after the God [[Zedancial]]. For unknown reasons, the [[Laughing Bloom]] also causes local fauna in the area to experience minor rapid growth. 

Because of the Pydnetta's absorption of large quantities of water, the window for agricultural activity has greatly increased in between wet seasons. 

## Since [[66 The Obsidian Eclipse (SAS)|The Obsidian Eclipse]]
Many travelers cross through the plains of Pydnetta as they travel between [[Diveneu]] and [[Aparinaskalio]]. Whether one is tired from their travels, stressed from life, or looking to turn over a new leaf, many found themselves accidentally exposed to the [[Laughing Bloom]] and found ample vegetation around the flower to consume.

As small communities began to form around patches of Pydnetta in the region, many began to notice effects of the [[Pydnetta]]. Soon, these communities began establishing successful farms in areas while enjoying a relatively relaxed life style.

Being an amalgamation of settlements, Pydnetta is not a centralized city but there are various hubs that have common services.

No one has been able to industrialize the plains. Any industrial tycoon that has sent workers to Pydnetta has found their work force succumbing to the easy going style of the locals
