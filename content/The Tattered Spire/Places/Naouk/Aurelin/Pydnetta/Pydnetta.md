The sprawling low land fields of Pydnetta is the home to many wayward vagabonds seeking to experience the Laughing Bloom. Famous for the Pydnetta flower that the city derives its namesake from, Pydnetta is less a city more than it is a ramshackle of settlements on stilts.

Despite the areas fertile soil, Pydnetta has been sparsely populated due to frequent storms from the west seas and runoff from the surrounding mountains that would flood the plains.
