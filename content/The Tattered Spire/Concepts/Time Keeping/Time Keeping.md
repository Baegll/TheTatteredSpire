The widely accepted calendar [[Calemyri]] was created and standardized by a wizard name [[Nessasarok Epagos]] from [[Abyrmyria]]. The [[Yehr]] consists of six [[Solams]], with a day of rest, celebration, or morning between each [[Solams|Solam]]. Each [[Solams|Solam]] is a time for worship of one divine aspect. They each have three [[Woxs]]. The first [[Woxs|Wox]] of any [[Solams|Solam]] is called [[Veyra]]. The second is called [[Kamerad]]. The third, [[Helve]]. In each [[Woxs|Wox]] there are two [[Daes]] dedicated to each of the three moons ([[Alvorden]], [[Neton]], [[Zeaf]]). The names of these [[Daes]] are as follows: [[Alvdae]], [[Netdae]], [[Zedae]], [[Vordendae]], [[Tondae]], [[Eafdae]].

[[Yehr|Yehrs]] are tracked since the [[Shattering of The Aegis]]. The peaceful adolescent world has its [[Yehr]] counted [[PAS|Prior Aegis Shattering (PAS)]] when [[The Aegis]] protected the world from extraplanar threats. Since [[Shattering of The Aegis|The Shattering]], [[Yehr]]'s are tracked [[SAS|Since Aegis Shattering (SAS)]]. Currently in the world it is [[205 The Current Year (SAS)]].
