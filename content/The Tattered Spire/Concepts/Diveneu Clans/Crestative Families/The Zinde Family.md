The family head, [[Zak'reth Zinde|Matron Zak'reth]], is rumored to have connections to the mysterious upper floors of [[The Tattered Spire]], though she neither confirms nor denies such claims. Their younger members often work as specialized guards for magical expeditions, particularly those involving potential planar rifts or dimensional instabilities. They show particular reverence for [[Thessarian]], [[Silviana]] and [[Poret]], viewing knowledge and fate as intertwined forces.
