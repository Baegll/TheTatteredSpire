[[The Crested Families]] are a collection the most powerful families who dominate [[Diveneu]]. While [[The Palestone Bloodline]] rules the city, these families frequently get into fights, despite their necessity of eachother as they grow ever-more specialized. The core system is tiered. The Bloodline being the primary family, followed by the Clans, and then notable Families. Smaller families vying for power are known as [[The Crestative Families]], nobles in their own right, but not seated in positions of real power.

The balance among Crested Families is profoundly unstable. [[The Stone War]] a stark reminder of the devastating power each family possesses. While the structure remained unchanged from the war, each family is constantly in a state of balancing. Alliances shift, rivalries simmer, and the threat of renewed conflict is ever-present. 

## [[The Palestone Bloodline]]
The original settlers of [[Diveneu]], this albino family line tapped and monopolized the power of [[Palestone]]. They are the family of power who all Crested families listen to. They created and maintain [[The White Roads]], live in the residential suites of [[The Tattered Spire]], and finished [[The Stone War]] with terrifying power. Some consider [[The Golzak Tribe]] to count as part of the Palestone bloodline, but [[The Crestative Families]] consider the Golzaks to be pets of the Palestones, and nothing more than commoners.
## [[The Duergar Clan]]
The Duergar were ambitious dwarves who live primarily in [[The Under-Diven]], their existence as city guards is tainted by their influence in [[The Stone War]]. Despite their reputation, they are one of the more powerful families of [[Diveneu]], denoted by their Clan status.
## [[The Bismuth Clan]]
This clan carved their legacy with their prodigious mining operations, particularly in [[The Diven Mountain Range]]  where [[Diveneu]] sits. They extracted vast quantities of rare minerals and gems. For centuries, the Bisumuths served as the primary suppliers to every crested family, and nearly every family who operated in [[Diveneu]]. 
## [[The Slagsmoke Family]]
In addition to being the primary swordsmiths, the Slagsmokes are the primary city guards of [[Diveneu]], combining their above-ground nature with [[The Duergar Clan]], they complete the citys defenses.
## [[The Gravesbones Family]]
Named for their creation and maintenance of [[The Catacombs]], this religious family is full of clerics and paladins, regularly patrolling the streets, [[The Catacombs]], and [[The Under-Diven]] sending the undead back to their graves.
## [[The Copper Pockets Family]]
Notably the only non dwarven Crested Family. The Copper Pockets made a fortune during [[The Mercantilism Marvels]] and opened the most successful banking chain in [[Aurelin]], with the original location in [[Diveneu]] named [[The Copper Pocket]].
