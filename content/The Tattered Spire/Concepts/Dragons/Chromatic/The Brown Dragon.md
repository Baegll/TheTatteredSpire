Brown dragons are exceptionally quiet, noted for their disdain of talking to creatures they call food. Their feathers are brown not just because of their color, but because of the mud caking their feathers to their hide as an additional protective layer. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is a sticky corrosive ooze of sludge which can be ignited into a blaze by the talons on their wings.

Brown dragons have the naming title "Nz"
