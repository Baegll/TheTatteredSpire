Yellow Dragons are secretive dragons often nicknamed the 'Salt Dragon.' Bearing a striking resemblance to [[The Green Dragon]], their feathers are yellow on one side, and blue on the other. Their unique ability to finely bristle and rotate their feathers allows them to effectively camoflage themselves as a green dragon. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is an [[Acidic]] spray of salt water, for which they get their nickname.

Yellow dragons have the naming title "Uc"
