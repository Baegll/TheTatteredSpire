White dragons are considered the weakest and most feral of [[The Chromatic Dragon|The Chromatics]]. Known for their intense hunger, they spend their days most commonly hunting and feasting. Their heads are thin and small, almost appearing as if they do not have a head at all just a single long neck. Their feathers are very thick, making them an exquisite choice for being turned into warm soft clothing.[^1] [[White Dragon Saliva]] is also highly prized as a natural liquid coolant. Due to these reasons, they are somewhat rare as they have been hunted near into extinction. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is extremely cold, well known to freeze their prey solid before they tear into and shatter the flesh of their prey.

White dragons have the naming title "Ga"
