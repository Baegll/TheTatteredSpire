Blue dragons have both blue feathers and a single long twisted pointing horn[^1]. Their tail is especially long and has a large number of feathers, those brave enough to mention it would call it fluffy before being smitten with lightning by the dragons' [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]].

Blue Dragons have the naming title "Ns"
