Red dragons are considered the most powerful of [[The Chromatic Dragon|The Chromatics]]. Most often associated with their insatiable lust for gold and wealth. They spend a vast amount of their time increasing the size and rarity of items in [[Hoard|their Hoard]]. Their ruby red feathers often have intricate patterns with varying shades of red covering them in interesting patterns. Their horns are swept back and often straight. They are a natural enemy of [[The Silver Dragon]], and their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is fire.

Red Dragons have the naming title "Ua"
