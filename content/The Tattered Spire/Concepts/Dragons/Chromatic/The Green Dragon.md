Green dragons are considered the second strongest of [[The Chromatic Dragon|The Chromatics]]. They have a large waving feathery fin that starts from their nose and trails down to their tail. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is an [[Acidic]] cloud of gas.

Green dragons have the naming title "Ib"
