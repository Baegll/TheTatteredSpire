Black dragons are the most vile-tempered and cruel of [[The Chromatic Dragon|The Chromatics]]. Easily distinguished by their pitch black feathers and their long curving horns which form a crescent shape pointing forwards. Their body is typically thinner and longer than other dragons. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is a powerful acid, capable of melting metal, flesh, and bone within seconds. It is also a potent antimagical solution utilized in potions.[^1] 

Black Dragons have the naming title "M"
