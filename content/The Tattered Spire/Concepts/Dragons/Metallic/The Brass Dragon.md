Brass dragons are often conversationalists. Often some of the best, and most willing, sources of advice in [[Naouk]]. Their outstretched wings form a distinct triangle shape as their wings are attached along its entire body to the tip of their tail. Their head is also of a unique shape, their singular horn forms a large curved plate which acts as a visor protecting its eyes before curving downwards into two sharp points acting like additional fangs. Their scales are a yellowish dull gold color, radiating heat. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is fire.

Brass dragons have the naming title "Zn"
