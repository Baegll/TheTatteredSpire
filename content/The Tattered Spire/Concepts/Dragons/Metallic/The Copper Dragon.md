Copper dragons are notable tricksters. Their green scales shimmer and often appear to change color, Newly hatched copper wyermlings are a muddy brown color which gradually shifts to a strong green as they age. Like the [[The Brass Dragon|Brass Dragon]], their wings are connected to its body all the way to the tip of the tail, however their wings have a sharp curve to them, forming a 'V' instead of a triangle. Copper dragons have two horns which are flat and extend horizontally from their head. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is [[Acidic]], and has a strange effect on time.[^1]

Copper dragons have the naming title "Cu"
