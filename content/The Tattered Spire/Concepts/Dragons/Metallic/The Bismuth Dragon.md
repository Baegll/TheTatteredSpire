Bismuth dragons are known for their multicolored scales and secretive nature. With horns of crystal jutting out at odd angles.[^1] They are typically wider than other dragons, and have shorter legs with sharp claws capable of cutting through stone with ease. Bismuth dragons are notable wizards and sorcerers, and the most studious of [[The Metallic Dragon|The Metallics]]. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is a powerful shock wave.

Bismuth Dragons have the naming title "Bi"
