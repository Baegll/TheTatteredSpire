Bronze dragons have blueish tinted scales. They have many spines, and many spikes resembling almost a porcupine. Their sense of duty is their defining trait, often called the first [[Paladin|Paladins]], these creatures take and uphold various oaths of duty and honor. Oathbreaking bronze dragons will shortly become ill and die after just a few [[Daes]]. [[Bronze Dragon Spikes]] are strong, often used in the forming of weapons.[^1] Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is lightning.

Bronze dragons have the naming title "Sn"
