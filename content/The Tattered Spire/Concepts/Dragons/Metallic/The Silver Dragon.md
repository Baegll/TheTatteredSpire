Silver dragons often form clans of other silver dragons, generally are considered the kindest of [[The Metallic Dragon|The Metallics]] and often form benefactory noble houses in popular cities. Their spreading of wealth makes them a natural enemy of [[The Red Dragon]]. Their white scales are very heavy, making them have impressively long wingspans. They also typically have large frills along their neck. Their mutli pronged horns are of smooth silver with black tips, and especially sharp. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is cold, often ice or a paralyzing gas.

Silver Dragons have the naming title "Ag"
