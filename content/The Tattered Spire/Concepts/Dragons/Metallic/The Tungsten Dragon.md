Tungsten Dragons, with a dull gray for scale color, and small vestigial wings, make up for this with their immense size. Being the physically largest of the [[The Metallic Dragon|Metallic Dragons]], they are grand in their own right. With large curved horns, they are easily able to dig and ram through solid rock. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is a blast of superheated and [[Bludgeoning]] sand.

Titanium Dragons have the naming title "W"
