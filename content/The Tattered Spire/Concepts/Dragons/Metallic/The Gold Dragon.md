Gold dragons are considered the most powerful of [[The Metallic Dragon|The Metallics]], and generally the most dedicated to learning and sharing knowledge. They spend most of their time shapeshifted into a humanoid form (typically elven) in libraries. Their golden scales shimmer brightly in the sunlight and are a prize to be sought after for armor. They are one of the few dragons who do not have horns. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is fire.

Gold Dragons have the naming title "Au"
