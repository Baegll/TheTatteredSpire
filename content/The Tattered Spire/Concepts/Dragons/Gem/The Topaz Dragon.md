Topaz dragons have golden-yellow crystalline scales that shimmer through various amber tones as they move. Their horns are perfectly symmetrical, curved, and their points touch forming a perfect circle as if they have a halo[^1]. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is a ray of necrotic energy which can disintegrate matter rapidly.

Topaz Dragons have the naming title "Az"
