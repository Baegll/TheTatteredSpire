Sapphire dragons have a brilliant blue crystal scale color. Their multiple horns are curved and swept into a graceful wave-like pattern. They are known for their calm wisdom and often act as oracles, their crystal scales[^1] showing visions of possible futures. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is a bolt of crystallized lightning.

Sapphire Dragons have the naming title "Ir"
