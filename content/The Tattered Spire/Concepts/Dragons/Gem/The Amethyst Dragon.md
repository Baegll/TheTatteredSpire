Amethyst Dragons are the most psychically gifted of [[The Gem Dragon|Gem Dragons]], with crystalline purple scales that seem to shimmer and pulse from an inner light. Their elongated skulls house powerful minds capable of telepathic communication across vast distances. Multiple small crystal horns crown their heads in geometric patterns[^1]. The [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is a wave of psychic force that can shatter minds as easily as stone. They are natural philosophers and often find opportunities to act as advisors.

Amethyst Dragons have the naming title "Am"
