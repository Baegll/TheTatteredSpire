Emerald dragons have deep green scales with a natural geometric faceting. Their horns grow into long perfect spirals and their eyes are a solid green. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is often a stream of liquid[^1] which quickly evaporates into a cloud of toxic gas that can cause tumors to grow at an incredible pace.

Emerald Dragons have the naming title "Ra"
