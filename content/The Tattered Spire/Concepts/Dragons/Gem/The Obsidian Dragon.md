Obsidian Dragons have a jet-black scale[^1] color that are razor sharp to the touch. Their bodies are sleek and angular. Steam often jets out from their scales due to their internal heat. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is superheated ash and glass shards. 

Obsidian Dragons have the naming title "Di"
