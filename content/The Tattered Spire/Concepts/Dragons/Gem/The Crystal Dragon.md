Crystal dragons possess translucent, prismatic scales that refract light into dazzling rainbow patterns. Their bodies appear almost glass-like, making them seem fragile despite their incredible durability. Sharp crystal formations jut from their spine and joints. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is a [[Radiant]] beam of light. They are well-known for their vanity and love of beautiful objects.

Crystal Dragons have the naming title "Ys"
