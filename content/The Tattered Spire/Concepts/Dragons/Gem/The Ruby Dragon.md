Ruby Dragons have red scales that drip hot blood[^1] which evaporates as it leaves their body. Their horns are trimmed into perfect gemstones by their ruby claws. They are known as the most emotional dragon with an increasingly hot temper, and a deep obsessive passion for control. Their [[The Breath Weapon|Breath Weapon]] is fire.

Ruby Dragons have the naming title "Ry"
