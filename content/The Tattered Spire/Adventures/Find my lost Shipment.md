# Locate and bring back my shipment of Spices
## Synopsis
The text on the post is finely written with magnificent penwork, but a bright red 'URGENT' written in dripping paint catches your eye. A local merchant known as [[Optimal Shadowbind|Opti]] is paying for someone to go to the last known location of his latest shipment of various spices from [[Acenus]]. If able to find these spices and bring them back, he will pay you for however much you bring back.
