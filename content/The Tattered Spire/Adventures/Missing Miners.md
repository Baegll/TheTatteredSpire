## Synopsis
A nearby iron mine in [[Mt. Oryndral]] has gone dark, we need a crew to go over there and figure out what happened in the deep. [[The Sanron Family]] has set up a hefty award for whoever can figure out what happened, and bonuses for any rare finds, or goods returned from the mine.
