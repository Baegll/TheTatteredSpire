#### Plot Points
The new party met at [[The Gilded Quill]] in the evening, after talking with [[Optimal Shadowbind|Opti]] they head to meet [[Rohla Ikot]] at [[The Bronze Courts]]. 

The party spots the party on [[The Quest for the Augg]], briefly talks to [[Mordreich au Kreig]] who tells them theyre on their way to [[The Beast & Bone]], the parties split somewhat amicably.

The party meets with [[Rohla Ikot]] at [[The Bronze Courts]]. She tells them where the document is, that there are new wards put in place, and eventually, that [[Optimal Shadowbind|Opti]] has her sister captive.

They party makes a plan that [[Rohla Ikot]] will leave a window cracked open to the third floor, where the document `847-B` is at, located in Maritime Section D-12.

The party snoops around the the archive, heads into the new head archivists [[Vorthak Lostar|Vorthak]] office.

The party knocks out a guard in the office. Leaving him nearly dead from the acid bolt. The find the backup document copies, 12 gold pieces, a book on [[The Panathedim#Femook|The Dewbringer]], and a used up infernal contract. They take a few moments to create a decent carbon copy of the document which contains mentions of [[Silk Root]].

After successfully nabbing the documents behind the wards the party makes their escape back to [[The Gilded Quill]], where [[Optimal Shadowbind|Opti]] pays each party member 60 gold, and send them on their way after learning how messy the operation was. As they leave, they spot [[Rohla Ikot]]'s sister tied up.
