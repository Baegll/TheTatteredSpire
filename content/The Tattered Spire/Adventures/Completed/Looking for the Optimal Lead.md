#### Plot Points
Mordreich sets out alone, discussing potential employment with [[Optimal Shadowbind|Opti]].
Learns two job leads. The first, killing the Head Archivist [[Vorthak Lostar|Vorthak]]. The second, stealing [[The Cloak of the Diplomat]] from [[The Goldleaf]] which is run by Madame [[Cordelia Ashworth]].
Is set up to work with [[Thymin Shadowbind]] a half-orc with a loud mouth on the assassination of Vorthak.
Heads to [[The Beast & Bone]] to talk with [[Caro Torken|Mr Torken]], Mordreich owes Mr Torken a job, in response he was given a nice deep blue cloak and some studded leather armor to wear for the day/job.
On his way home, Mordreich notices [[Prisca Turi]] (formerly Prisca Shadowbind) is following him. After a brief, tense, confrontation he manages to convince Prisca to work the job with him instead of Thymin.
In the morning, Mordreich heads to the smaller [[Temple of Thessarian]] for scoping it out. Meets with Prisca, and then when Vorthak enters, attempts assassination. Something stays his hand and lets full combat break out between Vorthak, a man he walked in with, and Prisca. After an changing of form into a dark smoke being, Vorthak is unsuccessful in fleeing.
After convincing the guards he is an adventurer who vanquished an evil thing, he and Prisca head to [[Optimal Shadowbind|Opti]] to give him an earful of choice words. Prisca relinquishes her name as a Shadowbind, and Mordreich forces Opti owe a valuable favor instead of taking any pittance of a gold reward.
