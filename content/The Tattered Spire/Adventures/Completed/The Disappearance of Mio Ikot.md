# Disappearance of Mio Ikot
## Synopsis
When the party last saw [[Rohla Ikot]]s sister, she was bound and gagged in the backroom of The Gilded Quill after being used as leverage by [[Optimal Shadowbind|Opti]] against Rohla to facilitate a document heist. Now, days later, disturbing rumors circulate through Diveneu's Copper District about screams heard from the tavern's upper floors and a strange absence of the Shadowbind family's usual activities.
